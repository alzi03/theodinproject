Lasagna Stuffed Garlic Bread Recipe

Lasagna Stuffed Garlic Bread is cheesy lasagna stuffed into garlicky, buttery bread creating the perfect dinner everyone loves!


Ingredients

  • 1 Family Size Angelo's Lasagna with meat sauce
  • 1 large loaf italian or french bread
  • 4 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 clove garlic, or 1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
  • parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Heat the lasagna in the microwave according to box directions
  2. Hollow out the bread leaving a good bit on the bottom for support.
  3. After the lasagna has cooled for 5 minutes place slices of it into the bread
  4. Mix the melted butter with garlic and brush onto bread
  5. Sprinkle everything with parmesan
  6. Bake for 8-10 minutes until golden
  7. Slice and serve
